# Mindspace

### Build your own corner of the internet.

The internet became feeds.

Feeds became algorithms.

Algorithms became performance.

Mindspace is an alternative.

Create a customizable digital home for your thoughts, ideas, photos, videos, essays, and creative work. Instead of posting into someone else's platform, build a place people can visit.

No followers.

No algorithms.

No pressure to perform.

Just your space.

---

## What is Mindspace?

Mindspace is a free template that helps you create your own personal website using GitHub Pages.

Think:

- MySpace
- Tumblr
- Digital Gardens
- Personal Blogs
- Online Journals

But simpler.

Your page can be whatever you want it to be:

- A collection of thoughts
- A visual moodboard
- A journal
- A creative portfolio
- A photo archive
- A digital scrapbook
- A public notebook

There are no rules.

---

## What Can I Customize?

Every Mindspace is unique.

Customize:

- Your homepage title
- Hero video or image
- Colors
- Typography
- Thoughts and quotes
- Photos and videos
- Navigation labels
- Layout and structure
- Personal bio
- External links

Make it feel like you.

# Getting Started

## 1. Create Your Own Copy

Click:

**Use this template**

at the top of this repository.

Create your own repository.

Example:

```text
janes-mindspace
```

---

## 2. Replace the Hero Video

Locate:

```text
hero.mp4
```

Upload your own video.

Ideas:

- Travel footage
- City lights
- Camera roll memories
- Creative projects
- Nature clips
- Personal moments

Keep it under 30 seconds if possible.

---

## 3. Customize Your Homepage

Open:

```text
index.html
```

Replace the title and description with your own.

Example:

```text
LATE NIGHT THOUGHTS

A public archive of private thinking.
```

Or:

```text
FIELD NOTES

Things I don't want to forget.
```

Or:

```text
SCRAPBOOK

Fragments of a life in progress.
```

---

## 4. Add Your Content

Open:

```text
thoughts.html
quotes.html
brain-dumps.html
```

Replace the sample content with your own.

Add:

- Thoughts
- Quotes
- Journal entries
- Ideas
- Observations
- Photos
- Links

Whatever matters to you.

---

## 5. Publish Your Site

Go to:

```text
Settings
→ Pages
```

Under:

```text
Build and deployment
```

Choose:

```text
Deploy from a branch
```

Then select:

```text
Branch: main
Folder: /(root)
```

Save.

GitHub will publish your site automatically.

---

## 6. Share Your Space

Your website will be available at:

```text
https://yourusername.github.io/repository-name/
```

Example:

```text
https://jane.github.io/janes-mindspace/
```

Send it to friends.

Post it online.

Or keep it just for yourself.
